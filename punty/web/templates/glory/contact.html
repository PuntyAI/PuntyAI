Frequently Asked

How do I reset my password?

Email us with your registered email address and we'll help you reset it.

Can I change my display name?

Yes! Just send us an email with your new preferred display name.

When do picks close?

Tipping closes before each race starts. We recommend getting your picks in early!

How are results determined?

We use official race results from Racing Australia. Results are typically entered within an hour of each race.

About Mat

Creator of Group One Glory

Mat Kerr is a lifelong racing enthusiast from Melbourne. After years of running informal tipping comps with mates via spreadsheets and group chats, he decided to build something better. Group One Glory is the result - a free, fun way to compete with friends over the biggest races of the carnival season.
