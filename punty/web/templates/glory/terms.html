4. Competition Rules

Picking:

  • You may select one (1) horse per Group 1 race
  • Picks must be submitted before the designated closing time for each race
  • You may change your pick at any time before tipping closes
  • If your selected horse is scratched, you must select a different horse before tipping closes

Scoring:

  • 1st place finish: 3 points
  • 2nd place finish: 2 points
  • 3rd place finish: 1 point
  • All other finishes: 0 points

Tiebreakers:

  • Total number of winners (1st place finishes)
  • Total number of placings (1st + 2nd place finishes)
  • Earliest registration date
