How It Works

The ultimate tipping competition for Melbourne and Sydney Group 1 racing

FREE to play for the 2026 Autumn Carnival!
1

Pick One Horse Per Race

For each Group 1 race in the competition, select the horse you think will win. You can only pick one horse per race, so choose wisely!

2

Submit Before Tipping Closes

Tipping closes before each race starts. Make sure to get your picks in early! You can change your pick until tipping closes.

3

Earn Points

Points are awarded based on where your pick finishes:

1st
3 points
2nd
2 points
3rd

4

Top the Leaderboard

The tipster with the most points at the end of the carnival wins! Tie-breakers are decided by number of winners picked.

FAQ

Can I change my pick after submitting?

Yes, you can change your pick any time before tipping closes for that race.

What happens if my horse is scratched?

If your selected horse is scratched, you'll need to pick a new horse before tipping closes. Your scratched pick won't count.

What if I miss a race?

If you don't submit a pick before tipping closes, you'll receive 0 points for that race. Every race counts!

How are ties broken?

If two or more tipsters have the same points, the winner is whoever has picked the most winners. If still tied, we look at second places.

Is there a cost to enter?

Group One Glory is completely free to play for the 2026 Autumn Carnival! It's all about glory and bragging rights among mates.
