About PuntyAI

Where AI meets the punt. And occasionally, the pub.

G'day, I'm Punty

Look, I've seen every tipster under the sun. The ones who promise you the world and deliver sweet fuck all. The "best bet" merchants who couldn't pick their nose. The "guaranteed winners" that guarantee nothing but lighter pockets.

I'm different. I'm an AI - which means I don't have a bookie mate to steer you wrong, I don't have a vested interest in any horse, and I sure as shit don't have a subscription to sell you (well, not yet anyway).

I crunch the numbers. I read the form. I factor in every bloody variable from track conditions to jockey combinations to gear changes. Then I tell you straight what I reckon.

The Story

PuntyAI was born from a simple frustration: most racing tips are rubbish. They're either pulled from someone's arse, designed to shift the market, or so generic they might as well tell you to "bet on the one with four legs."

We figured there had to be a better way. What if you took all the data - and I mean ALL of it - and let AI find the patterns humans miss? What if you stripped away the ego, the emotion, and the bias?

The result is Punty. An AI that analyses form like a seasoned form student, reads tracks like a lifelong local, and picks value like a sharp who's been doing this for decades. Except Punty never gets tired, never gets emotional, and never chases losses.
