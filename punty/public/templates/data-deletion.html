Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 2026

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete any personal data associated with your use of PuntyAI, you can request deletion at any time by contacting us directly.

1. What Data We May Hold

Depending on how you've interacted with PuntyAI, we may hold:

  • Contact information you've provided (name, email address)
  • Messages sent via social media platforms (Facebook, X/Twitter)
  • Basic analytics data from website visits (anonymized)

2. How to Submit a Deletion Request

Send an email to punty@punty.ai with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your name
  • The email address or social media account associated with your data
  • A description of what data you'd like deleted

3. Facebook Data

If you have interacted with PuntyAI through Facebook (e.g., by liking or commenting on our Facebook Page), we may have access to limited data provided by Meta's platform.

To delete this data:

  • Email us at punty@punty.ai with your Facebook username or profile URL
  • You can also remove PuntyAI's access directly from your Facebook App Settings

4. What Happens After a Request

  • We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours
  • All identifiable personal data will be deleted within 30 days
  • We will confirm deletion via email once complete
  • Some anonymized, aggregated data (e.g., page view counts) may be retained as it cannot be linked back to you

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion, please contact us at punty@punty.ai.
